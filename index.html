Colorado Hiking Club

Join us for an awe-inspiring Colorado sunset!

Or, a breath-taking view while snowshoeing in the Rockies!

There are many trails to choose from for every age, physical endurance, and interests. No matter the season in Colorado, hiking is popular whether in the summer or winter. For many, during snowy times, snowshoeing is subsituted for hiking as a more common form of exercise and means of experiencing nature.

Club members will find exhilerating hikes from the eastern to the western side of the state, to maintain your interest. A variety of photo opportunities are awaiting those who are sure to seek refuge in nature's playground!

The Colorado Hiking Club welcomes members of all walks of life. If you are interested in joining one of our groups, please fill out the information below and someone will be in touch.
